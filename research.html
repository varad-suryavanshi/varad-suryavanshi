Research Projects

High Frequency Trading Using Sentiment Analysis

Engineered a high-frequency trading (HFT) system that combines market indicators with real-time sentiment signals to improve trade accuracy and profitability.

  • Integrated technical indicators (MACD, RSI, Bollinger Bands) with Twitter and Google News sentiment scores.
  • Built an LSTM-based deep learning model for forecasting stock movement using combined features.
  • Conducted backtesting and hyperparameter tuning to optimize strategy risk-return profile.
  • Presented at: 8th International Conference on Data Management, Analytics, and Innovation (ICDMAI).

Tulsi Leaf Disease Detection Using CNN

Designed a deep learning system for identifying diseases in holy basil (Tulsi) leaves using CNNs and GANs for image classification.

  • Explored multiple architectures: VGG16, ResNet50, R-CNN, and GANs for performance benchmarking.
  • Performed advanced preprocessing: Gaussian blurring, HSV conversion, and color thresholding.
  • Achieved high precision, recall, and F1 scores via transfer learning and hyperparameter tuning.
  • Applied image augmentation and ResNet-based feature extraction to boost generalization.
