About Me

Hi! I’m Varad Vijay Suryavanshi—a passionate AI researcher, data analyst, and software developer currently pursuing my Master’s in Computer Science at New York University. I earned my Bachelor’s in Engineering from the University of Mumbai and have developed expertise in machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and systems programming.

My work spans a diverse set of projects—from fine-tuning large language models on Indian legal texts (ConstituAI) and building end-to-end document understanding pipelines to designing real-time data streaming systems and sophisticated predictive models for healthcare and finance. I interned at NTT Data Business Solutions, where I built an AI-driven chatbot that significantly improved search accuracy and reduced API costs.
